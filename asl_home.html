Origins of ASL

  • orginated in the early 19th century
  • there are many dialects of ASL, from different areas in the world
  • death education was introduced to the U.S. in 1814
  • ASL was influenced by French Sign Language

Fun Facts

  • about 70 million people in the world use sign language to communicate
  • ASL is the most widely used sign language
  • there are over 200 sign languages around the world
  • the sign for 'I love you' combines signs for the letters i, l, and y
  • apes can learn sign language and are able to communicate with humans using sign language
  • facial expressions define whether you are making a statement or asking a question
